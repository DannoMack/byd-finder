Dolphin is the kind of car that can turn curiosity traffic into leads because the price point is concrete. If BYD brings it to Canada near the mid-$30,000 range, it immediately becomes a cross-shop against the Leaf, not an abstract future model.

The weak point is still charging confidence. Leaf's CHAdeMO setup is aging badly in Canada, but BYD still has to prove Dolphin arrives with cold-weather equipment and enough dealer support to matter.
