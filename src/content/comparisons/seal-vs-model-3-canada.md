The Model 3 is the benchmark BYD has to beat in Canada, but the benchmark is narrower than it looks. Seal can win on upfront price and cabin value even if Tesla still keeps the edge on charging speed and network convenience.

For Canadian buyers, the real question is not which car wins a spec-sheet argument. It is which one lands with the better ownership math after provincial electricity rates, insurance costs, and winter losses are factored in.
