BYD is preparing four models for launch in Canada, sources close to the company have indicated. The specific models have not been confirmed publicly, but industry watchers expect the lineup to draw from BYD's globally successful models — likely including compact and mid-size offerings to cover multiple price points.

The company's Canadian launch is expected to begin with demo units in Toronto by mid-2026.
