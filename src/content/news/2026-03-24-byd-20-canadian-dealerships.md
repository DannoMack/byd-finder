BYD has accelerated its Canadian launch plans with a publicly stated target of up to 20 branded dealerships in its first year of Canadian operations. The company has engaged Dealer Solutions Mergers & Acquisitions — a Markham, Ontario-based automotive retail consultancy — to identify and secure retail locations across the country.

Initial focus is on three sites in the Greater Toronto Area, with planned expansion to Vancouver, Montreal, and Calgary in subsequent waves.

The 6.1% Canadian tariff on Chinese-built EVs stands in stark contrast to the 100% duty imposed by the United States, making Canada one of the most accessible developed markets for BYD's North American strategy.
