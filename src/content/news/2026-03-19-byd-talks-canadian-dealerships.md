BYD is in active discussions to establish a Canadian retail presence, industry consultants have confirmed. The company has registered production facilities with Transport Canada's Appendix G preclearance registry — a critical step toward import approval that no other Chinese passenger-vehicle manufacturer has yet taken.

Demo units are expected to begin arriving at Canadian dealerships by mid-2026, with limited retail availability projected for late 2026.
