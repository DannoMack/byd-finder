The BYD Sealion 7 is a mid-size electric SUV competing with the Tesla Model Y, Ford Mustang Mach-E, and Hyundai Ioniq 5. It's a newer model in BYD's lineup and has been rolled out to multiple international markets in 2025-2026.

## Why it's likely for Canada

- Mid-size SUV is the dominant EV segment in Canada
- AWD variant available for winter capability
- 82 kWh pack targets 500+ km real-world range
- Slots naturally between Atto 3 and larger family SUVs
