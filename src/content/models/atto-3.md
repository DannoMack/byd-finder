The BYD Atto 3 is BYD's global-success compact SUV and the model most commonly used to launch BYD in new markets. It competes in the Tesla Model Y / Hyundai Kona Electric segment.

## Why it's likely for Canada

- BYD's launch model in Australia, UK, Germany, Mexico, and Brazil
- Competitively priced for compact EV SUV segment
- 60 kWh Blade Battery (LFP chemistry, fire-safety advantage)
- Winter-viable range for Canadian climates

## What we're watching

- Canadian pricing (expected sub-$50,000 CAD to undercut Tesla Model Y)
- Cold-weather range retention on the Blade pack
- Whether BYD offers AWD variant for Canadian market
